CaptionForge AI ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AI Caption Generator & Hashtag Tool mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect
1 Advertising Data

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your device's Advertising ID to show personalized ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

2 Locally Stored Data

The App stores your saved captions locally on your device using your device's storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3 Usage Data

We do not collect any personal usage data, analytics, or crash reports directly. AdMob may collect anonymized usage statistics as part of ad serving.
